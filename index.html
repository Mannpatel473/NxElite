FAQs

Everything you need to know about working with NxElite.

What services does NxElite IT Solutions offer?
We offer Web Development, Logo Design, Brochure Design, Packaging & Printing, Business Card Design, IT Consulting, and an Internship Program. Every service is tailored to your specific business needs and goals.
Do you work with clients outside India?
Absolutely! We are a fully remote team and work with clients across India and internationally. All communication, deliverables and project management happen online — making geography a non-issue.
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by project scope. A logo design typically takes 5–7 business days; a website may take 3–6 weeks. During our initial consultation we'll define a clear timeline with key milestones and deliverables.
How do I apply for the internship program?
Send an email to Hr@nxelite.in with the subject line "Internship Application", along with your résumé and a brief note on your area of interest. Our team will get back to you within 48 hours.
How is pricing determined?
Pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity and timeline. After a free consultation, we provide a transparent, detailed proposal with no hidden costs. We're committed to delivering exceptional value for your investment.
